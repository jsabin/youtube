Instructions for accessing the 17th Ward Sacrament Broadcast

The broadcast can be viewed live and will be recorded for viewing later for a short period of time. Steps to access the ward broadcast are as follows:
  1. Go to YouTube on your computer or mobile device (see instructions below).
  2. Login to YouTube. Create an account if you don't have one.
  3. Go to the ward channel and click the subscribe button.
  4. You can now access the ward channel from any device by logging into YouTube and selecting the "Subscriptions" button from the navigation bar.

The following are instructions for accessing the ward YouTube channel from different devices.

  1. Click here to go to the 17th Ward channel.
  2. Select the live broadcast or the recorded video.

Samsung Smart TV

The first time you go to the broadcast you will need to add the YouTube app to your TV.

Add the YouTube App to your TV

  1. Click on the source button on your remote.
  2. Find and select the "Apps" option in the bottom panel of the screen.
  3. Select the search option (magnifying glass) in the top right corner.
  4. Type "YouTube" in the search.
  5. Select the YouTube app.

Find the 17th Ward YouTube channel

  1. Sign in to YouTube.
  2. Select "Subscriptions" from the navigation bar.
  3. Select the ward channel.
  4. Select the live broadcast or the recorded video.

Roku TV

The first time you go to the broadcast you will need to add the YouTube app to your TV.

Add the YouTube App to your TV

  1. Select the "Add Channels" option.
  2. Select "Search Channels" and type in "YouTube".
  3. Select "YouTube" and then "Add Channel".
  4. Enter your PIN when prompted.

Find the 17th Ward YouTube channel

  1. Sign in to YouTube.
  2. Select "Subscriptions" from the navigation bar.
  3. Select the ward channel.
  4. Select the live broadcast or the recorded video.
